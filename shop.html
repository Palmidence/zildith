Information Purchasing Information

Purchasing a rank on our servers is the best way to show your support if you want to help Zildith Network continue running.

If I purchase a rank, where will the money go to?
The money will directly go to supporting the server. This includes development of new features, server costs, and management.
When will my rank be activated?
Your rank should be processed within 24 hours. Any requested refunds will be provided if you have paid and you have not recieved your rank within the 24 hour period.
May I transfer my rank to another account?
Ranks may be moved and transferred to different accounts only if you have ownership of the account the rank is being transferred to.
How do I upgrade my rank?
We do not currently offer discounts for upgrading to ranks. However, you may purchase another rank on top of the one you currently have (e.g Gold -> Emerald will have both ranks active at the same time.) You will recieve the perks from the highest rank you have purchased.

Refunding Our Refunding Policy

Once purchased, ranks are non-refundable, unless you haven't recieved your rank within 24 hours. Do not issue PayPal chargebacks, we will assist you.


If you are having an issue with a rank, please let an administrator know by giving them your transaction number and any extra details.
